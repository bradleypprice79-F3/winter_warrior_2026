Leave Right — Discussion Guide

A facilitator-friendly guide to lead a discussion on constructing a legacy by Getting Right, Living Right, and Leading Right.

Review the oxygen-mask progression

  • Get Right: put your mask on first, so you can help those around you.
  • Live Right: be in proper relationship with others, help them with their mask.
  • Lead Right: develop the skills and virtues to better influence movement to advantage.
  • Leave Right: construct a legacy.

What Is a Legacy?

Legacy has two components:

  1. The Advantage you achieve for your Groups
  2. The development of other men

Legacy is what endures because of you, not what you did for yourself.

“Leave Right is the result of a lifetime of Mission accomplishment through Living Right and Leading Right, neither of which is possible unless a man has first Gotten Right.”

Questions

  • Have you Gotten Right?
  • Since there is no static plateau, how do you Stay Right over time?

The Ultimate Life Problem (ULP)

  • Problem: disadvantage that can and must be remedied
  • Condition: cannot be remedied
  • Deck Chair: can be remedied, but does not need to be

The ULP is not a Condition or Deck Chair.

Mission-building in 4 Steps

  1. Identify the ULP
  2. Devise a solution
  3. Map a course of action
  4. Enlist other men

Focus on step one: Identifying The ULP

“Every person is born with a ULP and identifying it is not very difficult.”

Discussion prompts:

  • Do you agree that this is not difficult?
  • What is your ULP?

Developing Virtuous Leaders (LDP)

Legacy is built primarily through the development of other leaders.

The 4 Phases of the Leadership Development Process

  1. Schooling
  2. Apprenticeship
  3. Opportunity
  4. Failure

Discussion

  • Which phase are you currently in?
  • Who are the men you are developing?
  • Where are they in the process?
Facilitator tip: keep the conversation practical—ask for examples, names, and next actions so men leave with a commitment they can act on.
